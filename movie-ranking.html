My Favourite Telugu Movies

My top Favouritetop Movies all time

1. RRR (2022)

Director: S. S. Rajamouli
Starring: N. T. Rama Rao Jr., Ram Charan, Alia Bhatt, Ajay Devgn
Plot: Set in the 1920s, RRR is a fictional story about two real-life Indian revolutionaries, Alluri Sitarama Raju and Komaram Bheem. It follows their separate journeys and eventual partnership against British colonial rule.
Why I like it: The action, emotional story, and powerful performances make it unforgettable. The song "Naatu Naatu" became a global hit.

2. Baahubali: The Beginning (2015)

Director: S. S. Rajamouli
Starring: Prabhas, Rana Daggubati, Anushka Shetty, Tamannaah Bhatia
Plot: A man named Shivudu discovers his royal legacy and the tragic history of his family in the kingdom of Mahishmati. The film ends with the iconic cliffhanger, “Why did Kattappa kill Baahubali?”
Why I like it: The visuals, story, and grand scale of the film made it a landmark in Indian cinema.

3. Arjun Reddy (2017)

Director: Sandeep Reddy Vanga
Starring: Vijay Deverakonda, Shalini Pandey
Plot: Arjun Reddy, a talented but hot-tempered surgeon, spirals into self-destruction after losing the love of his life. The film follows his emotional breakdown and slow recovery.
Why I like it: It’s raw, emotional, and the performance by Vijay Deverakonda is very intense and real.

4. Eega (2012)

Director: S. S. Rajamouli
Starring: Nani, Samantha Ruth Prabhu, Sudeep
Plot: A man is killed by a rich businessman and reincarnates as a housefly to take revenge. Despite the unusual concept, it's full of action, comedy, and creativity.
Why I like it: The idea of a fly as a hero sounds silly, but it’s done so well that it becomes fun and emotional.

5. Mahanati (2018)

Director: Nag Ashwin
Starring: Keerthy Suresh, Dulquer Salmaan, Samantha, Vijay Deverakonda
Plot: A biopic of legendary actress Savitri, showing her rise to fame, personal struggles, and tragic fall.
Why I like it: It's emotional, beautifully made, and Keerthy Suresh’s performance is outstanding.
